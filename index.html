Guannan Li

I am Guannan Li, a PhD candidate in Economics at McGill University, advised by Prof. Markus Poschke and Prof. Saraswata Chaudhuri. My research focuses on innovation and economic development, with particular interests in how technology affects productivity and the determinants of innovation.

My current research topics include innovation and regional development, star inventors and knowledge spillovers, and technology adoption and labor markets.
